# Current Officers (Spring 2026)
## Board of Directors
**See [minutes](https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~staff/bod/) for attendance.**

The Board of Directors is the decision-making entity behind the OCF's operations. The Board is appointed based on attendance at our [weekly meetings](https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/about/staff).

## General Managers
**Joseph Wang** \<storce>

**Rosarin Sanglimsuwan** \<rsang>

General Managers handle the organizational activities of the OCF and lead the Board of Directors meetings. They are elected by the Board of Directors at the end of each semester.

## Site Managers
**Sophia Williams** \<sbwilliams>

**Jaysa Garcia** \<jaysa>

Site Managers lie behind the technical work involved in maintaining the OCF lab and other services. They are elected by the Board of Directors at the end of each semester.
